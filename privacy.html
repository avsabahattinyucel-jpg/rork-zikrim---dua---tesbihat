Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Overview

Zikrim is a devotional and spiritual companion app that may provide dhikr, dua, tasbihat, prayer times, qibla, reminders, account features, premium subscriptions, cloud backup, and AI-assisted experiences. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed when you use the app and for what purposes.

Data We May Process

  • Account information such as email address, display name, and authentication provider details when you sign in.
  • App preferences such as selected language, theme, notification choices, and devotional progress.
  • Approximate or precise location data when you use prayer times, qibla, or nearby places features.
  • Subscription and entitlement status for premium access.
  • Cloud backup and restore records when you enable sync features.
  • Content you enter into AI-assisted or journaling areas.
  • Technical, security, and diagnostic records required for the reliable operation of the app.

Why We Process Data

  • To provide account sign-in, premium access, backup, restore, and personalization.
  • To deliver prayer times, qibla, reminders, widgets, and other app features.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and resolve technical issues.
  • To respond to support requests and operate AI-assisted features you explicitly use.

Third-Party Services

Zikrim may rely on third-party services such as Apple, Firebase, Google Sign-In, RevenueCat, Google Mobile Ads, push notification services, cloud infrastructure, and AI-related backend services. These providers may process limited data necessary to operate the relevant feature.

Payments and Subscriptions

Payment card information is not stored directly by the app. In-app purchases and subscription billing are handled through Apple App Store infrastructure and related payment systems.

Retention and Deletion

We retain data only for as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected, for legitimate security and backup needs, and where required by applicable law. If you delete your account, related data is removed or anonymized subject to technical and legal retention constraints.

Your Choices

  • You can deny camera, photo, notification, or location permissions at device level.
  • You can request account deletion from within the app when signed in.
  • You can manage subscriptions through your Apple account settings.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact support@zikrim.app.
